# Appointment Preparation

For a general checkup:
- No special preparation needed
- Bring a list of current medications
- Arrive 15 minutes early for paperwork

For lab work and blood tests:
- Fast for 8 to 12 hours before your appointment
- Drink water as normal
- Bring your insurance card and photo ID

For new patients:
- Complete the new patient form, available on our website or at the front desk
- Bring photo ID and insurance card
- Arrive 30 minutes early to complete paperwork

For existing patients:
- Confirm your appointment 24 hours in advance if possible
- Bring any referral paperwork if required by your insurance
- Update your contact information at the front desk if it has changed

If you need to cancel or reschedule, please call at least 24 hours in advance to avoid a no-show fee.
