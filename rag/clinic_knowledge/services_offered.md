# Services Offered

Sunrise Health Clinic provides the following services:

Primary Care:
- Annual physical exams and wellness checkups
- Sick visits for colds, flu, infections, and minor injuries
- Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension, asthma)
- Preventive care and vaccinations

Internal Medicine:
- Comprehensive management of complex or chronic conditions
- Cardiology referrals and follow-up care
- Diabetes and metabolic disorder management

Pediatrics:
- Well-child visits and growth monitoring
- Childhood vaccinations
- School and sports physicals
- Sick visits for children up to age 18

Dermatology:
- Skin condition diagnosis and treatment
- Mole and lesion evaluation
- Acne, eczema, and psoriasis management
- Prescription skincare treatment plans

Lab Services (on-site):
- Blood draws and routine bloodwork
- Urinalysis
- Rapid strep and flu tests
- EKG
