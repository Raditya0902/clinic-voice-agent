# Parking & Directions

Address: 4521 W Main Street, Suite 200, Tempe, AZ 85281

Parking:
- Free parking is available in the lot directly in front of the building.
- Additional parking is available in the covered garage on the east side of the building.
- Handicap accessible spaces are available near the main entrance.
- Parking validation is not required — parking is always free for patients.

By Car:
- From US-60 East: Take the McClintock Drive exit, head north, then turn left on W Main Street. The clinic is on the right side, about half a mile down.
- From US-60 West: Take the Rural Road exit, head north, then turn right on W Main Street. The clinic is on the left side after the traffic light.

By Public Transit:
- Valley Metro Bus Route 56 stops directly in front of the building on W Main Street.
- The closest light rail station is Tempe Transportation Center, approximately 1.2 miles away. Ride-share services are available from the station.

Building Access:
- The clinic is located on the second floor, Suite 200.
- Elevator access is available near the main lobby entrance.
- The building entrance is accessible for wheelchairs and mobility devices.
